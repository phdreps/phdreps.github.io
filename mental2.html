47%
Did you know that 47% of PhD students met criteria that classified them as depressed? (Check this cool poster!!)
Taking care of yourself can be really challenging while doing your PhD. All of us experience, in our own way, stress due to experiments not working, failing several goals you set for yourself, disappointments with career and impostor syndrome to name a few.
When we experience several of them repeatedly, it's hard to process the frustration and stress in a healthy positive way, and it can start to feel overwhelming. This can eventually lead to depression.
Finding yourself in this path is expremely normal and independent of age, background and lifestyle. We want to open the debate about mental health, and hot to take care of yourself while doing your PhD.
