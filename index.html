Let's talk about it.

In order to open the discussion, we will do a mental health awareness month in April. This includes:
  • Regular emails about this topic.
  • A pizza lunch with a discussion (15th April 12-14h save the date!!!)
  • Some information about the new appointed counsellor for the MDC and testimonies of people that go to counseling and how that improves their lives.
