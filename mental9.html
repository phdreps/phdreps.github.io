All good things come to an end, and the Mental health awareness series is one of them. It took us a little longer than we promised, but we believed the content was worth it and we wanted to do something valuable for the PhD community. I hope you enjoyed it :)

Take home message

Doing a PhD can push us to the edge, and that’s normal and happens to all of us. There are many ways of taking care of ourselves, from doing sports regularly to meditation, to meeting friends or seeing a therapist or a coach if we can’t cope alone. There’s no shame..

Feedback from the Pizza dinner

The pizza dinner was a success. We loved to organise and participate in it with you- thanks for joining! We learned from the feedback forms that you are motivated to attend events when there is an interesting program, and that you care about sharing coping mechanisms about mental health. We are motivated to continue the community by having more events like this soon. Spread the word!
